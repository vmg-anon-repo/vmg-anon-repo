=== Prompt ===
user: How much water do I need to drink every day?

=== Response ===
The amount of water you need to drink daily can vary based on several factors, including age, gender, activity level, health, and climate. A common guideline is the "8x8" rule, which suggests drinking eight 8-ounce glasses (about 2 liters) of water a day. However, some experts recommend men aim for about 3.7 liters (around 13 cups) and women about 2.7 liters (around 9 cups) per day, including fluids from all beverages and foods.

It's important to listen to your body and drink when you're thirsty, and adjust your intake based on your specific needs, such as increased physical activity or hot weather conditions.
