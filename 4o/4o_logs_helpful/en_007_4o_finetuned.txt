user: Can anyone learn piano?

=== Response ===
Yes, anyone can learn to play the piano! While some people might have a natural aptitude or previous experience with music, anyone can make progress with consistent practice and dedication. It's important to find a learning method that suits your style, whether that's through self-teaching, online courses, or lessons with a teacher. Remember to be patient with yourself and enjoy the process of learning and making music.
